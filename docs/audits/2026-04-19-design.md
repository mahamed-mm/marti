First dated design snapshot for Marti. Describes the screens as they actually compile today, not what the spec says. `docs/DESIGN.md` remains the lean intent; the older planning-era `intent-design.md` in this folder is the frozen starting point. Paired with `2026-04-19-architecture.md` written the same day.

## Visual identity

Dark-only, editorial. `MainTabView.swift:63` pins `.preferredColorScheme(.dark)` once at the root and nothing else flips it — the app cannot render light mode. Screens paint on `Color.canvas` (#010913) with surface steps (`surfaceDefault` → `surfaceElevated` → `surfaceHighlight`) carrying elevation instead of shadows on content. Cyan (`coreAccent` #84E9FF) is the sole primary accent — CTAs, active tab, verified glyph, selected price pin, range slider track. Gold (`statusWarning` #FEEB87) is reserved for rating stars. Pink (`statusDanger` #FF649C) is saved heart + error chrome.

Discovery list-mode leads with an editorial display title ("Feel at home.") in `martiDisplay` over a tall rounded search card (`DiscoveryHeroHeaderView.swift`), then a horizontal city-chip row and vertically stacked category rails of 170pt square-photo cards. Map mode strips the chrome down to three ultra-thin-material islands at the top (list toggle, live-count pill, filters) and a selected-listing card floating above the custom floating tab bar. The overall tone is restrained — few colors, generous spacing, glass islands instead of heavy panels, no decorative gradients on UI (LinearGradient appears only as a 24pt bottom fade on the list feed and a trailing fade on the city-chip rail, both purely functional).

### Typographic drift from the spec

- **`ListingCardView.swift:202`** uses `.font(.system(size: 14, weight: .bold))` on `Text(listing.title)` in the `.compact` card variant. This is the **only** text-level inline font-size in the codebase. `docs/DESIGN.md:71` says `.system(size:)` is permitted **only for SF Symbol icons** — this is a real drift. Closest token match is `martiLabel2` (`.footnote`, bold).
- **`SearchSheetView.swift:353-356`** defines a local `label(_:)` helper that composes `martiCaption.bold()` + `.tracking(0.5)` to render uppercase form labels ("WHERE", "CITY", "DATES", "GUESTS", "PRICE RANGE"). Matches the DESIGN.md uppercase-form-label rule, but the recipe isn't exposed as a reusable `Font` token or ViewModifier — it's inline inside the sheet. If any other sheet grows a form label, the recipe will get copied.
- Every other text callsite uses a `marti*` token. The 32 `.font(.system(size:…))` callsites across 18 files are all on `Image(systemName:)` glyphs (confirmed by inspection), which is the one permitted use.

### Off-scale literals

Seven hardcoded point values appear outside the scale:

| File:line | Value | Intent |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `ListingCardView.swift:54` | `.padding(.top, 14)` | full card title top padding |
| `ListingCardView.swift:64` | `.padding(.top, 10)` | full card location row |
| `ListingCardView.swift:75` | `.padding(.top, 6)` | full card rating row |
| `ListingCardView.swift:78` | `.padding(.top, 12)` | full card price row |
| `ListingCardView.swift:80-81` | `.padding(.horizontal, 14)` and `.bottom, 14` | full card content box |
| `SkeletonListingCard.swift:16-18` | `.padding(.horizontal/top/bottom, 14)` | skeleton mirrors the card padding |
| `DiscoveryHeaderPill.swift:19` | `.frame(height: 56)` | pill height (not in scale) |

`14pt` (six callsites) and `10pt`, `6pt`, `12pt`, `56pt` are all off-scale. `6/10/12/14` are 2pt-stepped — they read like a parallel "inside the card" rhythm inherited from `intent-design.md:99-100`: "Card content padding: 12-14pt horizontal, 12pt top, 14pt bottom". Two options: add `Spacing.cardPadding = 14` / `Spacing.cardTop = 12` (extend the scale) or rewrite the card to use `Spacing.base` + `Spacing.sm` combos. Right now the card and its skeleton hardcode the numbers twice — if the scale changes, they won't follow.

### "Arithmetic tokens"

Several callsites compose tokens with `+ 1 / + 2 / + Spacing.sm` to reach an in-between value:
- `VerifiedBadgeView.swift:53` — `Spacing.sm + 1` (= 5pt vertical padding on label capsule)
- `MapToggleFAB.swift:29-30` — `Spacing.base + Spacing.sm` (= 20pt), `Spacing.sm + 2` (= 6pt)
- `DiscoveryHeroHeaderView.swift:79` — `Spacing.md + 2` (= 10pt vertical padding on search card)
- `DiscoveryView.swift:280, 290` — `Spacing.md + Spacing.sm` (= 12pt)

These hint at four missing scale values: 5, 6, 10, 12, 20. A `Spacing.xs = 2, sm = 4, md = 8, md+ = 12, base = 16, base+ = 20, lg = 24` extension would absorb all of them.

## Corner radius

Token scale in `DesignTokens.swift:79-86`: `xs=4, sm=8, md=12, lg=24, xl=40, full=100`.

No raw `cornerRadius:` literals anywhere in the view layer — `rg 'cornerRadius:\s*\d+'` returns zero matches. Every `.clipShape(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: …))` and `RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: …)` passes a `Radius.*` token.

Observed distribution:
- `Radius.xs` — skeleton bars (`SkeletonListingCard:26`)
- `Radius.sm` — primary-button clip (`Buttons:24`), date pill (`SearchSheetView:226`), map-preview card photo (`ListingCardView:225`)
- `Radius.md` — every other card-like surface: full / rail / compact / map-preview cards, skeletons, empty-state container, filter sheet destination input, `MapListingsCarousel` wrapper
- `Radius.lg` — hero search card (`DiscoveryHeroHeaderView:82`), selected listing card (`SelectedListingCard:42`)
- `Radius.xl` — unused in views (only defined)
- `Radius.full` — `MapEmptyStatePill` preview stub

Capsule-shaped components use `Capsule()` directly rather than `RoundedRectangle(radius: Radius.full)`. No inconsistency — `Capsule` is the idiomatic SwiftUI primitive for pill shapes.

## Iconography

**SF Symbols: 100%.** Every icon is `Image(systemName: …)` — 31 callsites across 20 files. Zero raster `Image("…")` calls (confirmed by grep). App Icon lives in `Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/` but that's not referenced from any view.

Inventory of system symbols in use:

- Navigation/chrome: `magnifyingglass`, `slider.horizontal.3`, `list.bullet`, `map`, `map.fill`, `chevron.right`, `xmark`, `xmark.circle.fill`, `location.fill`
- Tab bar (`MainTabView:24-32`): `magnifyingglass`, `heart`, `calendar`, `bubble.left`, `person`
- Trust + state: `checkmark.seal.fill` (verified), `heart` / `heart.fill` (save), `star.fill` (rating), `person.crop.circle.badge.checkmark` (auth placeholder)
- Meta rows: `mappin`, `mappin.and.ellipse`
- Empty/error/offline: `photo`, `exclamationmark`, `wifi.slash`, `hammer` (coming-soon)
- Clustering: `square.stack.fill` (multi-pin indicator)
- Stepper: `minus`, `plus`

Stroke weight switches via `.font(.system(size:…, weight:…))`. Active tab is `.regular` / semibold is used for many glyphs regardless of state — `FloatingTabView.swift:150-154` uses `.regular` on both active and inactive tabs (only the label weight swaps between `.semibold` / `.medium`). `docs/DESIGN.md:101` explicitly permits this as a proxy for the 1.5pt vs 2pt stroke difference the spec calls for.

Each icon's sizing is one of `10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 16, 17, 18, 20, 26, 28, 32, 40, 56` points — functionally determined per context, not drawn from a shared icon-size scale. A small `Icon.xs/sm/md/lg/xl` enum would document intent (e.g. 12 = inline-meta, 17 = floating-button, 20 = hero-search, 32 = empty-icon), but the current free-pick is not actively hurting anything.

### Repeated recipes that could extract

- **"Glass disc" recipe** — 28pt or 44pt circle of `.ultraThinMaterial` + `Color.white.opacity(0.12)` hairline + drop shadow. Appears in `FavoriteHeartButton.swift:53-60` and `VerifiedBadgeView.swift:34-40`. Docstrings call this out as intentional ("the two top-corner overlays on a card read as a matched pair"), but neither file imports a shared primitive. A `GlassDiscBackground(size:)` `ViewModifier` would stop the two from drifting. The recipe also shows up at 44pt in `SelectedListingCard.closeButton` (`SelectedListingCard:237-241`) with a small variation (adds a dark `opacity(0.35)` scrim under the material) — three near-siblings of the same concept.
- **"Floating glass capsule/circle" recipe** — `.ultraThinMaterial` + `Color.dividerLine.opacity(0.25)` hairline + `Color.black.opacity(0.18) radius 8 y 2` shadow. Appears in `DiscoveryHeaderPill.swift:20-27`, `FloatingMapIconButton.swift:20-31`, `SearchThisAreaPill.swift:26-35`. Three files, identical recipe — a `FloatingIslandBackground()` modifier would centralize it. The recipe also re-appears with different shadow parameters in `MapToggleFAB` (solid fill, no material) and `MapListingsCarousel` card wrapper (opaque `surfaceDefault`, different shadow).
- **"Capsule hairline around a pin"** — `Capsule().stroke(Color.dividerLine, lineWidth: 0.5)` + `Color.black.opacity(0.25) radius 4 y 2`. Duplicated verbatim between `ListingPricePin.swift:47,56` and `PricePinCluster.swift:54,56`. Extracting a `PricePinBackground` would let both variants share the same visual grammar.
- **"Photo placeholder"** — `ZStack { Color.surfaceHighlight; Image(systemName: "photo").font(.system(size: 28)).foregroundStyle(Color.textTertiary) }` appears in `ListingCardView.swift:308-314` (size 28) and `SelectedListingCard.swift:124-131` (size 32). Not quite identical — sizes differ by callsite — but the concept is the same.
- **Uppercase form label** — `.font(.martiCaption.bold()).tracking(0.5).foregroundStyle(.textTertiary)` lives as a private `label(_:)` helper inside `SearchSheetView.swift:353-358`. If any other form sheet grows a label, this will get copied. Worth promoting to `Text(_:).style(.formLabel)` or a reusable helper.

## Accessibility coverage

- **Adjustable actions:** `PriceRangeSlider:36-40, 43-49` (both thumbs support `.accessibilityAdjustableAction`, announcing `$min` / `$max`).
- **Hidden decorative:** `SkeletonListingCard:22`, `SkeletonHeader:46`, `LoadingPinSkeletons:592`. Static skeletons are correctly hidden. `LinearGradient` bottom fades in `DiscoveryView` carry `.allowsHitTesting(false)` but no `.accessibilityHidden(true)` — inert gradients don't usually need it, but the decorative star / map-pin glyphs inside rating rows are **not** marked hidden individually; their surrounding container (`.accessibilityElement(children: .combine)` on the rail card at `ListingCardView:131`) rolls them up, which is correct.
- **Container combining:** `.accessibilityElement(children: .combine)` appears on the rail card (`ListingCardView:131`), hero header (`DiscoveryHeroHeaderView:51`), header pill (`DiscoveryHeaderPill:29`), verified badge (`VerifiedBadgeView:24`), and `MapListingsCarousel:71` uses `.contain`. `SelectedListingCard:48` uses `.contain` so its children stay individually navigable.

### Gaps

- `OfflineBannerView` (`ErrorStateView:41-54`) — the banner's text `"No connection"` is already readable, but there's no `.accessibilityAddTraits(.isStaticText)` or container grouping. VoiceOver reads the `wifi.slash` icon name + "No connection" as two separate elements.
- `ErrorStateView` at `ErrorStateView:3-38` wraps its own VStack but doesn't set `accessibilityElement(children: .combine)` — the ring circle + exclamation + title + message end up as four elements instead of one digest.
- `EmptyStateView:17-73` — same pattern, four separate VoiceOver stops (icon, title, subtitle, button) with no container roll-up.
- `MainTabView`'s `ComingSoonView` (`MainTabView:67-86`) — placeholder, no a11y.
- Coarse count by file: all 21 view files that carry interactive content have **at least one** a11y modifier; the container-combining discipline is uneven but no interactive element lacks a label.

## Drift from `docs/DESIGN.md`

- **`surfaceElevated` hex** — `intent-design.md:18` says `#243346`, `DesignTokens.swift:8` ships `#1F2D42`. `docs/DESIGN.md` (the lean spec) doesn't enumerate the hex; it says "use the token." The runtime value and the planning-era hex have diverged.
- **Unused tokens** — `corePrimary`, `statusSuccess`, `starEmpty` defined in `DesignTokens.swift:26-34` but have no callsites. `docs/DESIGN.md:17-34` only lists the tokens that are actually used.
- **Empty-star rendering** — the rating row draws `Text("New")` when `averageRating == nil` (`ListingCardView:70-73`) instead of five empty stars. Not a drift against the lean spec (which doesn't prescribe a "no rating" state), but `starEmpty` was defined for the originally-planned five-star outline that never shipped.
- **Card text** uses inline `.font(.system(size: 14, weight: .bold))` at `ListingCardView.swift:202` — the one real typographic drift in the repo.
- **Card inner padding** — 14pt / 10pt / 6pt / 12pt are hardcoded (`ListingCardView:54-81`, `SkeletonListingCard:16-18`) and don't live in `Spacing`. `docs/DESIGN.md` §Spacing says "if a value isn't in the scale, add it to the scale — don't reach for raw literals."
- **Animation curves** — `docs/DESIGN.md:196-202` names three canonical curves and says "don't add new ones." Code uses eight. Three of those (`.spring(0.35, 0.85)`, `.spring(0.4, 0.86)`, `.spring(0.45, 0.85)`, plus `.easeOut(0.4)`, `.easeOut(0.6)`, `.smooth(0.35)`, `.default`) are not on the allowed list.
- **Shadow tokens** — spec defines three recipes, code spells out 13 one-off recipes. No `Shadow.*` enum exists.
- **Reduce Motion coverage** — spec says "every animation should respect `@Environment(\.accessibilityReduceMotion)`." About half of the animation callsites do.
- **Hero search pill → card** — `intent-design.md:213-214` called for a 48pt `xl` radius pill. The shipped hero is a tall 76pt `lg` radius rounded card (`DiscoveryHeroHeaderView:79-89`). The lean `docs/DESIGN.md` reflects this evolution implicitly ("hero search capsule") but the component is no longer a pill — it's a card. Intentional redesign; noting as drift vs. the planning-era spec.

## Inconsistencies found

1. **Three near-identical "glass disc" backgrounds** — `FavoriteHeartButton.swift:53-60`, `VerifiedBadgeView.swift:34-40`, `SelectedListingCard.swift:237-241`. Same `.ultraThinMaterial + white @ 0.12 hairline + dark shadow` skeleton, three different shadow magnitudes (`radius 3 y 1` vs `radius 4 y 1` vs `radius 4 y 2` + extra scrim). Extract a `GlassDisc(diameter:)` view modifier with a single shadow recipe.
2. **Three near-identical "floating island" backgrounds** — `DiscoveryHeaderPill.swift:20-27`, `FloatingMapIconButton.swift:20-31`, `SearchThisAreaPill.swift:26-35`. All three draw `.ultraThinMaterial + Color.dividerLine.opacity(0.25) hairline + Color.black.opacity(0.18) radius 8 y 2 shadow`. Duplicated verbatim. Extract a `.floatingIslandBackground()` modifier.
3. **13 distinct shadow recipes** across 11 files, no `Shadow` tokens. Promote three canonical recipes (`.glassDisc`, `.island`, `.floatingCard`) into `DesignTokens.swift` and force callsites to pick one.
4. **Eight animation curves** in use where the spec permits three. Three unique springs with different response/damping. Promote three canonical curves into a `Motion` enum in `DesignTokens.swift` and replace `.default` / the six off-spec tunings with one of them.
5. **Off-scale padding literals** (14, 12, 10, 6) in `ListingCardView:54-81` and `SkeletonListingCard:16-18`. Either add `Spacing.cardPadding = 14, Spacing.cardRowGap = 6` etc., or rewrite the card to use `Spacing.base/sm/md` combos.
6. **One text-level inline font size** — `ListingCardView:202` uses `.font(.system(size: 14, weight: .bold))` on the compact-card title. Replace with `martiLabel2`.
7. **Three unused color tokens** (`corePrimary`, `statusSuccess`, `starEmpty`) in `DesignTokens.swift:26-34`. Either route a callsite to them (Verified label could use `statusSuccess`; Detail screen's "0 reviews" could render `starEmpty` outlines) or delete them.
8. **`Color.dividerLine.opacity(0.25)` hairline recipe** duplicated across `DiscoveryHeaderPill:24`, `FloatingMapIconButton:24`, `SearchThisAreaPill:32`. A `Color.softHairline` alias or a ViewModifier would cover it.
9. **`Color.white.opacity(0.12)` glass-disc hairline** duplicated across `FavoriteHeartButton:57` and `VerifiedBadgeView:37`; `white @ 0.03` on `VerifiedBadgeView:58` is a neighbor without a home. Both disappear into the glass-disc modifier from #1.
10. **Uneven VoiceOver container combining** — `ErrorStateView`, `EmptyStateView`, and `OfflineBannerView` render as 4 / 4 / 2 separate VoiceOver elements respectively instead of one digest each. Add `.accessibilityElement(children: .combine)` to the root VStacks.

## Recommended cleanups (prioritized)

1. **Extract `Shadow` tokens.** Add a `Shadow` enum to `DesignTokens.swift` with at least `.glassDisc`, `.island`, `.floatingCard`, `.tabBar` and a `.shadow(_ token:)` helper. Replace all 13 ad-hoc `.shadow(color: …, radius: …, y: …)` calls. This also lets `FavoriteHeartButton` stop drifting from `VerifiedBadgeView`.
2. **Extract a `glassDisc(size:)` modifier and a `floatingIsland()` modifier.** Replaces six files' worth of duplicated `.ultraThinMaterial + hairline + shadow` chrome and makes the two floating-island families (`FloatingMapIconButton`, `DiscoveryHeaderPill`, `SearchThisAreaPill`) impossible to drift.
3. **Move card padding into tokens, or rewrite the card to use the scale.** Six callsites hardcode 14pt. Either add `Spacing.cardPaddingH = 14`, `Spacing.cardPaddingTop = 12`, `Spacing.cardMetaGap = 10/6/12` and use them, or collapse the card to `Spacing.base/sm/md` combos. Fix `ListingCardView.swift:202` to use `martiLabel2` while you're there.
4. **Enforce the three-curve motion policy.** Add `Motion.uiSpring`, `Motion.quick`, `Motion.chromeHide` tokens and strip out the six off-spec tunings + `.default`. Add a `.respectingReduceMotion(_:)` modifier so half-the-codebase Reduce-Motion adherence becomes 100%.
5. **Decide on the three unused color tokens.** Either drop `corePrimary`, `statusSuccess`, `starEmpty` from `DesignTokens.swift` (and from the lean `DESIGN.md` if referenced there), or commit them to callsites (`statusSuccess` on a Verified label variant, `starEmpty` on a 0-rating five-star outline).

## Diff vs prior audit

No prior dated design audit exists — `docs/audits/` contains `intent-design.md` (the v0 planning spec, frozen) and `intent-architecture.md`, plus `2026-04-19-architecture.md` written today.

Since `intent-design.md`, the codebase has diverged in four material ways: (1) the hero search component went from a 48pt `xl`-radius pill to a tall `lg`-radius rounded card with an editorial display title above it; (2) the map surface was redesigned around three floating material islands + a bottom carousel/selected-card — none of which existed at intent time; (3) the `Spacing` scale grew three new entries (`screenMargin`, `cardGap`, `peekWidth`, `railCardWidth`) to codify rail geometry; (4) three planning-era tokens (`corePrimary`, `statusSuccess`, `starEmpty`) were defined but never wired to a callsite.

## Summary

**Top 3 inconsistencies:**

1. **No shadow tokens** — 13 distinct recipes across 11 files, with neighbors (`FavoriteHeart` vs `VerifiedBadge`) 1pt off from each other without a reason. Ground truth for elevation is spread across individual callsites.
2. **"Glass disc" and "floating island" recipes are copy-pasted** across six files (three disc callsites, three island callsites) with subtle drift in shadow magnitude and stroke opacity. A single ViewModifier for each would prevent further drift.
3. **Off-scale literals in the card** — 14/12/10/6pt hardcoded in `ListingCardView` and its skeleton, plus one inline `.font(.system(size: 14))` on text. The card is the most-rendered surface in the app; it shouldn't be the one place the token system leaks.

**Do this first:** ship `Shadow` tokens in `DesignTokens.swift` and a `.floatingIslandBackground()` view modifier. Those two alone collapse ~50 lines of repeated chrome into one place, make future theme tweaks one-line, and stop the "nine out of thirteen shadows appear exactly once" problem from growing with each new floating control. The card padding cleanup (#3 above) can wait until the next card variant ships — right now it's two files.
